Steak Recipe

Steak is a timeless dish that highlights the natural flavor of high-quality beef, often cooked to perfection with a crispy, flavorful crust and a tender, juicy interior. Whether you prefer it rare, medium, or well-done, the key to a great steak is selecting the right cut of meat, seasoning it well, and cooking it with precision. Popular cuts include ribeye, filet mignon, and New York strip, each offering a unique balance of tenderness and marbling.

To prepare steak, the meat is typically seasoned with salt, pepper, and sometimes garlic or herbs, before being seared in a hot pan or grilled to your desired level of doneness. Many steak lovers add a finishing touch, such as a pat of herb-infused butter, to enhance the flavor.
